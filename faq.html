FAQ

What's a hackathon?

A Hackathon is an event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers and project managers, collaborate intensively on software projects.

Who should attend?

You! If you have any interest in programming our making thins in general then this is for you!

I'm a beginner. Should I skip this?

No way!! No matter your skill level, you will be able to contribute to your team. If you're a little nervous we be having a meetup prior to the hackathon where a hackathon veteran discusses her experiences and offers tips for your first hackathon!
